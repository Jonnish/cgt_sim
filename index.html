Cancer vs. Treatment — a Game-Theory Simulator

A turn-based game built on Staňková, Brown, Dalton & Gatenby, “Optimizing Cancer Treatment Using Game Theory,” JAMA Oncol. 2019. You are the oncologist (the leader); the cancer is the follower that can only adapt to drugs you have already played.

Your two drugs

Effectiveness = max fraction of a cell type killed at full dose (before resistance). Combo multipliers apply to that drug’s effectiveness and toxicity when both drugs are given the same turn.

Max tolerable toxicity per turn = 4.0 (= the higher of the two base toxicities).

The tumour

Set global dials. Per-type counts, proliferation and resistance are derived from them (shown live on the right).
